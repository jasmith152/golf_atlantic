<p><font color="#000000"><font size="2"><b>How It Works</b></font></font></p>
<ul>
    <li><font color="#000000"><font size="2">The Railside Car Shaker is designed to deliver high amplitude, low frequency, linear vibration into the railcar. </font></font></li>
    <li><font color="#000000"><font size="2">Vibration transmitted in a plane perpendicular to the flow of the bulk material is transferred into the material more effectively than other methods. </font></font></li>
    <li><font color="#000000"><font size="2">This results in the most efficient and effective material movement possible. </font></font></li>
    <li><font color="#000000"><font size="2">The operating principals of the design allow for efficient influence of material in the rail cars with low stress to the rail car structure.</font></font></li>
    <li><font size="2">The NAVCO&reg; Railside Car Shaker is available in both pneumatic and electric models. </font></li>
    <li><font size="2">The Roller Railside is manufactured with heavy duty steel rollers at the contact point for continuous unloading of captive cars.</font></li>
